#### Overview

After the platform implementation phase, the project has demonstrated a DevOps workflow from local environments to AWS. However, the current scope is mainly suited for **technical demonstration, practice, and reporting**, and does not yet fully meet the requirements of long-term operations.

This section presents three topics in a structured way: **(1)** remaining limitations after the current phase; **(2)** proposed development solutions; **(3)** expected benefits upon implementation. This structure clarifies the rationale for the proposal before moving into architecture and implementation details in later sections.
