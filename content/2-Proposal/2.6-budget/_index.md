#### Overview

Expansion proposals may **increase operating costs** compared with the current demo environment. Budget should therefore be analyzed by item, enabling only components truly needed for the evaluation phase, and always including alert mechanisms and teardown after lab completion.

The FinOps principle applied is: **estimate before — monitor during — clean up after**.
